Pronto Cafe

Photoshop, Indesign, Illustrator

Pronto Cafe is a hip, university-based coffee shop that offers not only baked goods and coffee, but also entertainment. The cafe is targeting college students from the ages of 18 years old to 24 years old. The aim was to create fun and clever designs such as the moustache, nerd glasses, and hat patterns to be used diverse on the various merchandise. I also used different terms for the cup size, roast, flavor and dessert to implement the college vibe further.

Chicagoly Winter Edition Persona Drawings

Illustrator

For the Chicagoly Winter 2017 edition, an article on Chicagoan's perspective on the city was written. However, the head shots given weren't cohesive. The solution was to vectorize each headshot to look like rough drawings. By Using Adobe Illustrator and a brush, I vectorized portraits for the Chicagoly Magazine to give it a rough drawn look. The portraits were published in the Winter 2017 edition. I feel this project exhibits my talent in digital illustration.
